Spinach Dip

5	Servings
13.3 	ounce	sour	cream
0.8 	cup	mayonnaise
8.3 	ounce	frozen	chopped	spinach,	thawed	and	drained
3.3 	ounce	water	chestnuts,	drained	and	chopped
1.5 	ounce	dry	leek	soup	mix
0.8 	pound	sourdough	bread

Directions
Step 1 - Mix sour cream, mayonnaise, spinach, water chestnuts, and dry soup mix together in a medium bowl. Chill in the refrigerator 6 hours, or overnight.
Step 2 - Slice off the top of the sourdough round and pull out the soft interior, leaving a sturdy bread bowl. Fill with spinach mixture. Tear removed bread chunks into pieces for dipping.
